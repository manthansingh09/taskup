Privacy Policy

1. Overview

Welcome to TaskUp Global. TaskUp Global ("we", "us", "our") provides business process outsourcing (BPO), remote staffing, and virtual support services. We are committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of your personal data, company information, and client files.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, process, transfer, and disclose information from visitors to our website, clients who register for our services, and candidates applying to join our vetted talent network. By accessing our website or contracting our specialized support professionals, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect information through multiple channels during your engagement with TaskUp Global:

  • Client Profile Details: Full name, corporate email address, business phone number, company name, industry vertical, and estimated operational scaling scope provided via quote or scheduler forms.
  • Transactional Records: Billing address, payment verification metrics, bank routing details, and monthly SLA accounting data.
  • SOP & Workflow Data: Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), portal credentials, calendar inputs, business logs, files, and spreadsheets shared with your assigned remote specialists to complete tasks.
  • Applicant Information: Resume data, aptitude test logs, cognitive test metrics, software proficiency checklist entries, and background check records for candidates in our screening pipeline.
  • Automated Device Details: IP addresses, browser specifications, page access tracking, device details, and site usage statistics collected through session cookies and tracking tags.

3. How We Use Data

TaskUp Global processes your information for the following business purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To pair your company with specialized Virtual Assistants, Bookkeepers, Developers, or Marketers, and support onboarding integrations.
  • Customer Correspondence: To deliver project proposals, schedule risk-free trials, coordinate operational kickoff calls, and provide weekly performance logs.
  • Billing & Administration: To verify payment credentials, process subscription agreements, and enforce SLA deliverables.
  • Quality Assurance: To monitor task logs, compile platform diagnostics, and ensure high operational standards.
  • Legal & Safety compliance: To prevent fraudulent logins, resolve system security breaches, and comply with regulatory requirements.

4. Data Safeguards

We maintain comprehensive security measures designed to prevent unauthorized disclosure, destruction, alteration, or access of client files and data:

All TaskUp Global specialists are trained in data protection best practices. Remote workers connect through secure Virtual Private Networks (VPNs) and access client portal environments using multi-factor authentication (MFA). Client files, databases, and operational spreadsheets remain confined to secure cloud databases with strict access-control protocols.

In addition, all remote professionals execute strict data-privacy Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) prior to client assignment, ensuring zero unauthorized copying or sharing of proprietary workflow details.

5. HIPAA & Medical Data

For healthcare institutions, clinics, and medical practices requiring patient coordination or billing assistance:

TaskUp Global operates as a Business Associate under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). We implement rigorous physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect Electronic Protected Health Information (ePHI). Our healthcare virtual assistants complete extensive HIPAA compliance training, and we gladly sign standard Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) to solidify data-privacy alignment.

6. Third-Party Disclosures

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information or company database records to third parties. We share data only under the following conditions:

  • Service Providers: With trusted software utilities facilitating our workflow, including payment processors, calendar aggregators, and email distribution servers, all of which are bound by strict data confidentiality agreements.
  • Supabase Integration: We utilize secure Supabase environments for managing leads, quotes, and client bookings.
  • Legal Requirements: When compelled by regulatory authorities, court orders, or state/federal laws to safeguard security, prevent fraudulent transactions, or defend our legal rights.

7. Your Legal Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may hold specific statutory rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access & Correction: The right to view and correct personal records logged in our databases.
  • Deletions & Erasure: The right to request the deletion of non-essential profile details from our storage disks.
  • Opt-Out Options: The right to opt out of promotional newsletters or analytical site-tracking tools.

To exercise any of these rights, please email a formal request to our operations center.
